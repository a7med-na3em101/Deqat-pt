Who We Are

DEQAT AL-KAFAA is a specialized provider of accredited proficiency testing (PT) schemes designed to support laboratories and institutions in improving the accuracy and reliability of their test results. Our programs are conducted in compliance with ISO/IEC 17043.

Accreditations & Standards

Our commitment to quality and international standards

ISO/IEC 17043:2023 Compliant

Our PT schemes are designed and implemented in accordance with the international standard for proficiency testing providers. We also align with related standards including:

  • ISO/IEC 17025:2017
  • ISO 15189:2022
  • Industry-specific regulations

Participation in our programs helps laboratories meet their accreditation requirements and demonstrate technical competence.

Benefits of Proficiency Testing
